# Changelog

All notable changes to the E-Sevai SaaS Platform project will be documented in this file.

---

## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-18

### Added
- **Authentication & Authorization**: Supabase JWT validators, user invitations onboarding, and staff deactivation.
- **Center & Services Catalog Management**: Center onboarding approval workflows and multi-fee structural catalogs.
- **Application Processing**: APP-YYYY-000000 sequential mappings, document checklists, and SLA target due dates.
- **Document Management**: Multi-size limits, extension blocklists, SHA256 checksum integrity tracking, and private bucket uploads.
- **Payment & Receipts**: Snapshot caching, versioned receipt text generation, and 5-min signed URLs.
- **Notifications**: Categories, priority tiers (`low` to `critical`), and channel mock senders.
- **Analytics & Reports**: In-memory group compilers and multi-format exporters (CSV, Excel/TSV, and emulated HTML/PDF layouts).
- **Hardening & Observability**: Correlation IDs, in-memory rate limiters, environment validators, custom security headers, and expanded `/health` checks.
- **Deployment & Configs**: Docker multi-stage containerizations.
